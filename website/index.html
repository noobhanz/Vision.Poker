Questions

Is this allowed by poker sites?
Vision is a calculator that displays mathematical information. It doesn't automate actions, read memory, or interact with the poker client. It reads the screen and shows you the math.
Which poker sites are supported?
Works with PokerStars, GGPoker, PartyPoker, 888poker, WPT Global, and most other clients. Custom calibration available for unlisted sites.

Currently Mac only. Windows version is in development and coming soon. Join the waitlist to be notified.
How accurate is the equity calculation?
We run 5,000 Monte Carlo simulations per calculation, giving accuracy within 1%. Calculations complete in under 100ms.
Can other players see the overlay?
No. The overlay only appears on your screen. It's a transparent window that sits on top of your poker client.
